Our Foundation

Built on Love, Driven by Purpose

We are the Felix Family - a testament to the power of love, unity, and shared purpose. Five individuals, one unbreakable bond.

Founded on the principles of integrity and excellence by our parents, Felix Ordu Loveday and Victoria Ginikachi Felix, who laid the foundation for everything we are today.

To build a legacy that transcends generations - one rooted in faith, strengthened by education, and amplified by the success of each family member.

Our Future

Through unwavering support for one another's dreams and collective dedication to excellence, we're creating a future worthy of the foundation our parents built.
