About

Birthday: March 11
Known As: Chizim, Chiboy, Ezeamama (A Known King)
Education: BSc Biochemistry, University of Port Harcourt (2019) | MSc Biochemistry, Uppsala University (2025)

The firstborn son of the Felix family, carrying the weight and honor of the family name with pride and determination. Named Ezeamama by his grandmother, meaning "A Known King," he embodies leadership and vision for the future.

His academic journey took him from the University of Port Harcourt where he earned his BSc in Biochemistry in 2019, to Uppsala University in Sweden where he completed his Master's degree in Biochemistry in 2025. This international experience has shaped his worldview and prepared him for global excellence.

Driven by ambition and guided by the values instilled by his parents, he represents the next generation of the Felix legacy. With unwavering faith and strong family bonds as his foundation, he is building a future that honors the past while creating new paths for generations to come.
